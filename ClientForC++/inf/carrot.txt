It is necessary to start watering carrots immediately
after sowing to create optimal conditions for seed
germination and maintain moisture of the soil before
the formation of the root crop at the level of no
below 80% of the HB, from the beginning of formation
of root crops until the end of the growing season - 
not lower than 70% of HB. 14 - 20 days before 
harvesting root crops, watering stop
