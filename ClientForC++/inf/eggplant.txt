Optimum conditions for growth and crop formation
Eggplants are formed when the soil is moist the 
period from planting plants to setting of fruits
not lower than 80% of HB in a layer of 0 - 30 cm 
and 85 - 90% in the 0 - 40 cm layer during the 
fruiting period. The highest intensity of moisture
consumption eggplant is observed during the setting
period fruits - at the beginning of fruiting.
