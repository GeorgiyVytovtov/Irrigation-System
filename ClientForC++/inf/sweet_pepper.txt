Optimal conditions for growth and formation a good
harvest of sweet pepper are made at soil moisture
in the period from planting seedlings before fruit
setting 80 - 85% of the HB in layers 0 - 30 cm and
85 - 90% - in the layer 0 - 40 cm in the period fruiting.
The critical period of increased requirements of the
plant to provide moisture quite long (1.5 - 2 months):
from the beginning of the appearance the first buds to
set and grow fruits.
